Account & Data Deletion

If you want to request deletion of your account or associated data for King's Rules: Strategic Ludo or other Rockshell Studios games, please follow the instructions below.

📧 Email Request 🗑️ Data Deletion 🎮 Game Account Support

How to request deletion

Send an email to: gadesesamuel35@gmail.com

Use a subject like:

Account Deletion Request - King's Rules: Strategic Ludo

Please include

  • Your player name or in-game name
  • The Google Play Games account or email used with the game, if applicable
  • Any details that help us identify your data correctly

What data may be deleted

  • Profile information associated with your game account
  • Stored gameplay-related data connected to your profile
  • Chat-related records, where applicable
  • Other user data stored by Rockshell Studios for game functionality

What may be retained

Some limited information may be retained where required for legal, security, fraud prevention, or technical reasons.

Deletion timeline

Once we receive a valid request and verify the account, deletion requests are typically processed within 7 days.
